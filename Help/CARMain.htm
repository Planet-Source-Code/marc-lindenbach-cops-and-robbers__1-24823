The Survival Game

The Survival game is played by trying to get rid of all the helicopters in the air, to do this we use guns, nitros, and health (See Items). Also every second you last against the helicopters you gain a point. Once you have destryoed all the helicopters you will win the game, but if the helciopters destroy you first you will lose (See Helicopters)

The Robber Game

In the Robber game you are to get to the bank, and then get back to your club house (See Terrain) without getting killed. Unlike the survival game points are hard to come by, because you do not get a point every second. Besides that this game is much like survival execpt that you do not need to kill all the helicopters to win.

The Items

There are many items in this game they are:
The Mine: This is somthing that you do not want to step on if you do your car will loose 10 health pts.
The Health: This is a good thing, if you run over it you get 10 extra health points.
The Gun: This is a good thing, it lets you blow up a helicopter by clicking on it.
The Nitro: This is a good thing it lets you go 4x your regular speed for a short time by pressing the "space" key.
But be warned that somtimes these mines can be hiding under health boxes, guns and even nitros.

The Terrain

In Cops and Robbers there are 3 main different types of terrain they are road, buildings, and flower gardens, there are also two special ones they are the clubhouse and the bank (See Robber Game) Now below you can see each type of terrain, and what it does:
Building You cannot go through this type of terrain, but helicopters can fly over it.
Flower Gardens You can go onto this type of terrain, and helicopters can fly over it.
Club House This is where you start off and where you bring the money once you have stolen it from the bank.
Bank This is the object where you steal the money if you are playing in "Robber" mode.
The Tunnel There are two of these on the map, if you go into them you can end up on the other side of the map (hint: this is good for tricking or hearding off the helicopters)

The Options

Cops and Robbers is a very customizable game. When you are in the game click on the options menu and you will be showen a list of many things that can customize the game to your likings. The first thing you see is the name box, in this you can enter you name for later use. You will also see a health box in this you can pick how much health you want. You will also see a player speed and a helicopter speed box, you can use these to customize the speed of the helicopter and you. You will also see a checkbox that says unlimited helicopters besid it. This is a mode of play that when you blow up a helicopter a new one will respawn on one of the other helicopter pads.

The Controls

The Controls are quite simple for Cops and Robbers. They are the "up button" for going up, the "down button" for going down, the "right button" for going right, the "left button" for going left, the space bar for nitros, and "d" for the cheat menu(See Cheat Codes). When you have picked up a gun(See Items) You can click on on of the helicopters and blow it out of the air, depending on wether you have unlimited helicopters on (See Options) you may have one less helicopter left to deal with.
